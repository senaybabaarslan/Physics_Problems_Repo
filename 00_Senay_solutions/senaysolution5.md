## Problem 5 – Why do things turn? (Curvature)

**Ellipse:** $x=a\cos t, y=b\sin t$
1. **Normal Acceleration ($a_n$):** This is the force pushing you "sideways" to make you turn.
   $a_n = \frac{ab}{(a^2\sin^2 t + b^2\cos^2 t)^{1/2}}$
2. **Radius of Curvature at the tip ($t=0$):**
   $$R = \frac{b^2}{a}$$
   **Interpretation:** A smaller $R$ means the turn is sharper! If it's a circle ($a=b$), then $R=a$.
